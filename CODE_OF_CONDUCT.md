# Code of Conduct

PlayPen contributors are expected to keep the project useful, respectful, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about technical risks.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Prefer actionable suggestions over dismissive criticism.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption of discussion or project work

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the repository owner or a listed maintainer privately with relevant context and links. Before a public release, maintainers should add a dedicated conduct reporting channel here.
